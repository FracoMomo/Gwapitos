Introduction

The community we chose is Lawaan in Talisay, specifically the Mananga River. The water is really polluted and really dirty, there is some debris that might have been caused by Odette

The Interview

We will interview someone who lives in that area.

The questions

1. As you see the current state of the river, do you think it is still possible to clean the river?

2. Do humans contribute more waste to the river than natural causes?

3. What are some possible things you can contribute to help clean up the river as a bosconian?

Waste Manage Plan

1. We can still clean it up through people's awareness and more trash pickups.

2. Stopping contamination from water by improving waste collection from the area.

3. Create a program or event for promoting cleanliness around the area and spread more awareness.

Language varieties

In this community, most of them speak Bisaya and English,
a little bit of Tagalog that they use to communicate with each other.

Observed culture of the community:

People from that community are a bit nonchalant but they see trash and think about it,
but they're the type to just look at it and not really do something to clean it up

Achievement in total documentary production:

I learned a lot from this Documentary, it's that garbage is a common problem in the streets of the Philippines and governments
can't really manage most of it and this is the sad truth we have to face today.
There are some people who are really doing something for it but not enough,
so we need more people like them. Spreading Awareness is key.
